Welcome to Belampar Primary School!

For more than eight decades, our school has been a place where curiosity is nurtured, dreams are encouraged, and values are built for life. We believe every child has unique potential, and through a blend of academic excellence, cultural pride, and community spirit, we guide our students from Grade 1 to Grade 10 toward becoming confident, responsible, and capable individuals. Here, learning is not just about books—it’s about growing together as a family.

About Us:

Founded in 1937, Shree Belampar Prathmik Shala is a co-educational government school located in Belampar village, Mahuva taluka, Bhavnagar district, Gujarat (Pin Code 364290). We proudly serve students from Grades 1 to 10, providing them with a strong academic foundation and the skills to succeed in higher education and life. Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • Providing quality education aligned with the Gujarat State curriculum
  • Encouraging holistic development : academic, cultural, and physical
  • Promoting values of respect, responsibility, and community service
  • Preparing students for future studies and career opportunities

Our Community:

Located in the heart of Belampar, a close-knit rural community with a rich cultural heritage, the school plays a vital role in raising literacy levels and fostering growth among local children. Many of our alumni have gone on to higher education and professional success, carrying forward the values they learned here.
